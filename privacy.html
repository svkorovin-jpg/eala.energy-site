Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

eala energy ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the UK GDPR and EU GDPR.

1. Who we are

eala energy Ltd, Rex House, 4-12 Regent Street, London, SW1Y 4PE, United Kingdom.
Contact: info@eala.energy

2. What data we collect

Contact form: When you submit our contact form we collect your name, company, and email address in order to respond to your enquiry. This data is processed by Formspree (formspree.io) on our behalf.

Analytics cookies: With your consent, we use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymised data about how visitors use our website (pages visited, time on site, approximate location). This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

No other data: We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or sell data to third parties.

3. Legal basis for processing

Contact enquiries: Legitimate interest (responding to your message).
Analytics: Consent (you can withdraw at any time by clearing your browser cookies and declining on your next visit).

4. How long we keep your data

Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months. Analytics data is retained for 14 months per Google Analytics default settings.

5. Third-party processors

Formspree — processes contact form submissions. Privacy policy.
Google Analytics — website analytics. Privacy policy.
GitHub Pages — website hosting. Privacy policy.

6. Your rights

Under UK/EU GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, email info@eala.energy.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at eala.energy/privacy.
